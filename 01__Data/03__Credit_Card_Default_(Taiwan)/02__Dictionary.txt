LIMIT_Bal = Amount of the given credit (in dollars) - includes both the individual consumer credit and his/her family (supplementary) credit.

SEX: 1 = male; 2 = female

EDUCATION: 1 = graduate school; 2 = university; 3 = high school; 4 = others

MARRIAGE: 1 = married; 2 = single; 3 = others

AGE: In years

PAY_(0 --> 6): History of past payment --> We tracked the past monthly payment records (from April to September, 2005) as follows: 
	PAY_6 = the repayment status in September, 2005 
	PAY_7 = the repayment status in  August, 2005
	PAY_11 = the repayment status in April, 2005
	
	The measurement scale for the repayment status is:
			-1 = pay duly; 
			1 = payment delay for one month; 
			2 = payment delay for two months
			3 - 9 =  payment delay for (3 - 9) months

BILL_AMT(1-6): Amount of bill statement (in dollars). 
	BILL_AMT1 = amount of bill statement in September, 2005
	BILL_AMT2 = amount of bill statement in August, 2005
	BILL_AMT6 = amount of bill statement in April, 2005

PAY_AMT(1-6): Amount of previous payment (in dollar). 
	PAY_AMT1 = amount paid in September, 2005
	PAY_AMT2 = amount paid in August, 2005
	PAY_AMT6 = amount paid in April, 2005

Default payment next month: 0 = yes; 1 = no
